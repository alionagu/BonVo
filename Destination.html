Duft Museum Cologne

The Duftmuseum (Fragrance Museum) in Cologne is a captivating place that immerses visitors in the rich history of perfume making.
Located in the heart of Cologne, it showcases artifacts, historic bottles, and original equipment from centuries of fragrance craftsmanship.
An ideal destination for culture lovers, history buffs, and curious families!

  • Hands-on perfume exhibits
  • Located in central Cologne
  • Family-friendly and educational
